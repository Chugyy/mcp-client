ACME Corporation - Company Policies

Remote Work Policy:
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full remote work requires manager approval and must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance.

Vacation Policy:
- New employees: 15 days per year
- After 3 years: 20 days per year
- After 5 years: 25 days per year
- Unused vacation days can be carried over up to 5 days maximum.

Equipment Policy:
All employees receive a company laptop (MacBook Pro or ThinkPad, depending on role) and have access to a $500 annual budget for accessories like monitors, keyboards, or standing desks.

Training Budget:
Each employee has a $2000 annual budget for professional development including conferences, courses, and certifications.
